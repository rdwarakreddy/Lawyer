Leadership & Vision

Vinu & Associates is a Bengaluru-based firm of practicing Company Secretaries with a branch office in Kochi, Kerala. The firm delivers integrated solutions across corporate law, SEBI regulations, FEMA, NBFC advisory, valuation, corporate governance, and regulatory compliance.

With a multidisciplinary approach and deep industry expertise, the firm supports startups, SMEs, listed companies, multinational corporations, trusts, and investors through every phase of the business lifecycle.
